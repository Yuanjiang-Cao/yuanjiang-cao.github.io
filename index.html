I am a postdoc at the Macquarie University, Sydney Australia. I am supervised by Prof. Lina Yao and Prof. Michael Sheng. I achieved my Ph.D. degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of New South Wales (UNSW), supervised by Prof. Lina Yao and Prof. Wenjie Zhang. My research interests include generalization in reinforcement learning, generative models, domain generalization, adversarial robustness.
